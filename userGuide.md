# Activa Propiedades - Website User Guide

**Website Purpose:** Professional real estate, investment, and technical services platform offering property listings, investment opportunities, and comprehensive real estate solutions in multiple languages and currencies.

**Access:** Public website (no login required for browsing)

## Powered by Manus

**Technology Stack:**
- Frontend: React 19 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS 4 + shadcn/ui components
- Backend: Express.js with tRPC for type-safe API procedures
- Database: MySQL with Drizzle ORM for schema management
- Authentication: Manus OAuth integration
- Deployment: Auto-scaling infrastructure with global CDN

## Using Your Website

### 1. Browse Properties (Buy & Rent)
Click "Comprar y Alquilar" (Buy & Rent) in the header navigation. You can:
- Toggle between "Buy" and "Rent" options
- Search by location using the search bar
- Filter by price range (Under €500K, €500K-€2M, Over €2M)
- View property details including bedrooms, bathrooms, and square footage
- Switch between m² and sq ft using the unit selector in the header
- Convert prices to your preferred currency (EUR, USD, GBP, CHF) using the currency selector

### 2. Sell Your Property
Navigate to "Vender Propiedad" (Sell Property) to:
- Submit your property details through the valuation form
- Provide property information (address, type, bedrooms, bathrooms, square meters)
- Enter your contact information to receive a professional valuation
- Our team will contact you with a market assessment

### 3. Become a Real Estate Agent
Visit "Agente Inmobiliario" (Real Estate Agent) to:
- Learn about career opportunities with Activa Propiedades
- View benefits including global network access and professional development
- Submit your application with your experience level and background
- Join our international team of professionals

### 4. Learn About the Company
Click "Empresa" (Company) to:
- Read about Activa Propiedades' 20+ years of experience
- Discover our core values: Excellence, Global Reach, Teamwork, and Innovation
- View company statistics and achievements
- Understand our commitment to customer satisfaction

### 5. Find Our Offices
Select "Ubicaciones" (Locations) to:
- View all office locations (Madrid, Barcelona, Málaga, Valencia)
- Access contact information for each office
- Find office hours and directions
- Contact your nearest office directly

### 6. Save Favorite Properties
Click "Lista de Deseos" (Watchlist) to:
- View your saved favorite properties
- Remove properties from your list
- Quick access to properties you're interested in

### 7. Contact Us
Navigate to "Contactar" (Contact) to:
- Send a message directly to our team
- Select your inquiry subject (Buy, Sell, Investment, Technical Services, Other)
- Provide your contact information for follow-up
- Use the WhatsApp button for instant messaging

## Managing Your Website

### Language & Currency Settings
- **Language Selector** (top-right header): Choose from Spanish (ES), English (EN), French (FR), or German (DE)
- **Currency Selector** (top-right header): Switch between EUR, USD, GBP, CHF - rates update daily
- **Unit of Measure** (top-right header): Toggle between m² and sq ft for all property listings

### WhatsApp Integration
- **Floating WhatsApp Button** (bottom-right corner): Click to open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message in your selected language
- Available on all pages and mobile-friendly
- Direct connection to +34 642 383 997

### Admin Features (via Management UI)
- **Dashboard**: Monitor website analytics and performance
- **Database Panel**: Manage properties, projects, and testimonials directly
- **Settings**: Configure website title, logo, and visibility
- **Secrets**: Manage API keys and environment variables securely
